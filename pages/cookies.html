Cookie Statement of Better Housing

Better Housing B.V. & affiliated companies

Like most websites, Better Housing uses cookies — small data files that help improve your browsing experience and ensure our website functions optimally. This statement explains what cookies are, how and why we use them, and how you can control or delete them at any time.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer, tablet, or smartphone when you visit a website. These files contain data that allows a site to recognize your device on future visits.

For example, cookies can store your login information, language preferences, or site settings to make your next visit smoother and more personalized.

2. How long do cookies stay active?

Each cookie has its own lifespan. Some cookies (called session cookies) are temporary and automatically disappear once you close your browser. Others (persistent cookies) remain on your device for a set period or until you manually delete them. These longer-term cookies help the website remember your preferences for future visits.

3. Why does Better Housing use cookies?

Cookies serve several important purposes on our website. Specifically, we use them to:

Enhance your browsing experience and make our website easier to use;

Analyze how visitors use our site so we can improve its performance and usability;

Remember your settings and preferences, so you don’t have to re-enter them each time;

Offer personalized content or advertising that better matches your interests.

We never use cookies to collect sensitive personal information, and we never sell cookie data to third parties.

4. Types of cookies we use

A. Persistent cookies

Persistent cookies help Better Housing recognize you when you return to our website. This allows us to remember your preferences — such as language or cookie consent choices — so you don’t have to configure them again. These cookies remain active on your device until they expire or you delete them manually in your browser settings.

B. Session cookies

Session cookies keep track of your activity during a single visit. For example, they allow us to understand which pages you view and how you navigate the website. These cookies are automatically deleted once you close your browser.

C. Tracking cookies

With your consent, we may place tracking cookies on your device. These cookies help us and our advertising partners understand which pages you’ve visited and what interests you most, so we can provide more relevant ads and recommendations.

Tracking cookies never contain directly identifiable information (like your name or email). Instead, they build an anonymous usage profile. You can disable or remove these cookies at any time through your browser settings.

D. Third-party cookies

Some cookies on our site are placed by third parties, such as analytics providers or social media platforms. These cookies may track your activity across multiple websites to show you relevant content or advertisements. You can also manage or block these cookies directly through your browser or the third-party’s privacy settings.

5. Google Analytics

We use cookies from Google Analytics, a service provided by Google Inc., to collect anonymous data about website usage. This helps us understand how visitors interact with our site and how we can make it better.

Google may share this information with third parties if required by law or if third parties process the information on Google’s behalf. We have a data processing agreement with Google and have taken steps to anonymize your IP address to protect your privacy.

You can learn more about Google’s data policies here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Social media cookies

Our website may include buttons or links that connect to social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, or X (formerly Twitter). These features may place cookies from the respective platforms to enable functionalities like sharing or liking content.

Since these cookies are managed by third parties, Better Housing has no control over how they collect or use your information. We recommend reviewing the privacy and cookie policies of these platforms to understand how they handle your data.

7. Managing or deleting cookies

You have full control over the use of cookies on your devices. You can:

Accept or decline cookies when prompted on our website;

Delete existing cookies through your browser settings;

Configure your browser to block cookies automatically or notify you before storing one.

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of our website. For example, saved login information or site preferences might be lost, requiring you to re-enter them on your next visit.

8. Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction, or deletion of personal data collected via cookies, where applicable. For questions or requests regarding your privacy or cookie settings, you can contact us at: info@better-housing.nl

9. Updates to this Cookie Statement

Better Housing may modify this Cookie Statement periodically to reflect changes in technology, regulation, or our services. The latest version will always be available on our website. We recommend checking this page occasionally to stay informed.
